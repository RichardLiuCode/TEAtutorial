TEA Format

What is TEA Format

TEA format is a method to answer a question cleary. A TEA format contains 3 sentence which is the topic, evidence and Analysis.

  • Topic: The main idea you are talking
  • Evidence: The fact or example to support why your main idea is ture
  • Analysis: Explain how the evidence support your toplic

Example of TEA format

PBL is benefical for student. According to the statistics, student in PBL classrooms scored 15% higher on standarized scientests than students in traditional classrooms. This shows that PBL is benefical for student.
